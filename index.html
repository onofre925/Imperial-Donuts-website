About

Imperial Donuts is an independently owned and family run business that specializes in the creation of artisan donuts. Established in early 2014, we have been blown away by the unexpected success and popularity of our carefully crafted donuts. Our donuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-rise and amongst our most popularity donuts and our iced-glazed donut.

Currently we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily-basis and have two stores in the Brentwood area, one store in Montreal and one store in Chicago. Definitely pop by one of our locations to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it - don't worry! We've recently introduced both a catering service for those special occasions such as weddings & birthdays and have also recently intrduced a delivery service for our 'Surprise Donut Collection' box which contains 6 different donut flavors.
